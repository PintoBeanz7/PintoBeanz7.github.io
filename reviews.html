whats new

Comfort in Chaos

John Summit

Rating: ★★★★☆

As we all know, John Summit is currently one of the most successful DJs and producers, and he just released his debut album to make his run even stronger. Comfort in Chaos showcases his talent with as an album that highlights both new tracks and reimagines previous singles. He confidently expands beyond his typical tech house and house styles, incorporating influences from drum and bass, garage, and trance.

Inhale Exhale

Rufus Du Sol

Rating: ★★★★★

Overall, Inhale / Exhale is a captivating and cohesive album that highlights RÜFÜS DU SOL's skill in merging electronic music with deep emotional resonance. The powerful sound reflects their talent, making it a heartfelt tribute to both longtime fans and newcomers. It's a spiritual masterpiece from start to finish.

Bloom

Ben Bohmer

Rating: ★★★☆☆

Ben Bohmer returns with a stunning melodic house record that invites listeners to float away in its ethereal soundscapes. This new release not only rivals his earlier work but often surpasses it with catchier songwriting and even more immersive production. Each track is designed to calm the soul, making it a perfect soundtrack for relaxation or introspection.

Jamie XX

Waves

Rating: ★★★☆☆

Waves showcases a balance between the perfectionist vision of its creator and the vibrant, free-spirited energy Smith aims to revive in dance music. The album opens with "Wanna," featuring atmospheric tones that complement strings and vocal samples. The lead single, "Baddy on the Floor," highlights intricate techniques, allowing the percussion and horns to shine during its most energetic moments.
