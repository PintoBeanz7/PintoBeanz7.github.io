About Us

Our Philosophy

At The World Studios, our goal is to connect EDM fans with the sounds they crave. We believe in music as a universal commoditity that yields community and freedom. Our goal is to bring that feeling to a social platform that allows users to download, interact, and curate their own experience.

Who We Are

Founded in 2024, The World Studios is a team of avid EDM enthusiasts whose goal is to curate the best playlists, review the latest albums, and feature up-and-coming artists. We come from all walks of life and career backgrounds to ensure a diverse and inclusive approach to music. Ultimately, our mission is to create an engaging space for EDM fans to discover new music.

Join Us

Whether you're an artist looking for exposure or a fan searching for your next favorite track, The World Studios is the place for you. Join our community today and immerse yourself in the world of electronic dance music.
